Giving Future Sound & Vision its own identity allowed the company to visually distinguish itself from its subsidiaries. This took the form of a redesigned logo, brand guidelines and a website landing page to provide a simple portal for clients to quickly share the companies' distributed brands.

Creative Direction:

LOGO REDESIGN & BRAND GUIDELINES

I created a refreshed logo, applying a modern take to the original design which had remained unchanged since 1998.

The form encapsulates the audiovisual industry, combining a pupil with sound waves inspired by Chladni experiments.

The accompanying brand guidelines outline the company purpose, history, fonts, imagery and colour palette. 

Example: Chladni Plate

Extracts From Brand Guidelines:

LANDING PAGE REDESIGN

Future Sound & Vision had a URL, but no landing page. I generated a mock-up template using Adobe XD for the web development team to deploy. 

The template utilises a grid pattern so the page remains easy to maintain as new brands are onboarded.
