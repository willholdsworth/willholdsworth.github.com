I reinvigorated the Izabelle Hammon brand with revitalised imagery, compelling graphic design and a new product catalogue. These tools gave the national B2B network the tools they needed to effectively promote new quarterly campaigns and  other product releases to their clients.

Campaign Design:

'CAPTURE THE MOMENT'

This photography themed AW17 collection required eye-catching campaign imagery to promote the upcoming release. I created posters that were shipped alongside the collection when it was released so B2B clients could actively promote the new colourways as soon as they arrived.

I took the opportunity to capture high-resolution packshot imagery of the iZ bottles so they could be shared by the social media manager when the collection launched. 

Imaging Strategy:

STREAMLINING PHOTOGRAPHIC PROCESSES

Creating efficient imaging workflows reduced the teams' workload. This allowed us to dedicate more time to conceptualising future campaigns without needing to rephotograph identical elements for each campaign.

I created templates so new colours could be composited into bottles to simplify the process of adding new collections to the website and catalogues.

Proposal Design:

ELLISONS
PITCH DECK

Following the Creative Directors' brief, I created a pitch deck that outlined key Izabelle Hammon product categories.

This clean and minimal document clearly outlined the USP's of each range through the use of illustrated icons and concise copy. 

The document could be delivered as an in-person presentation, or as a physical deliverable. This allowed the creative director to email the pitch deck ahead of her meeting with the Ellisons team to negotiate the proposal.

Campaign Conepts:

'BELLE OF THE BALL'

I prepared initial campaign concepts for the upcoming SS18 campaign, bringing a bold and luxury aesthetic to the collection. 

Students attending Prom would be looking for a mature and elegant polish to match their outfits.

The campaign concept  was therefore crafted to appeal to 16-18 year olds who would be leaving school in the coming quarter. 

Graphic Design:

SOCIAL POSTS

Creating dedicated content for Instagram helped drive awareness of current campaigns in an engaging way with a clear and distinctive style that matched the company aesthetic.
