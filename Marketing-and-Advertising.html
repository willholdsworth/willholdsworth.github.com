I conceptualise and deploy creative strategies for large and small scale print, social media and digital campaigns to help brands showcase their products and services in a memorable way.

FUTURE AUTOMATION PRINT ADVERTISING

PROJECT SCOPE

Future Automation wanted to offer an 'on-demand' advertising design service so their 5000+ B2B dealer network could request print-ready artwork tailored to the products they sold, and the magazine they were purchasing their ad spot in.

I was tasked with generating a series of adverts that could be customised and resized to suit the magazine specifications that clients would submit. Creating customisable templates ensured an efficient and high-quality output for clients who didn't have the additional marketing budget to hire a freelance designer.

CREATIVE STRATEGY

Creating a tagline with a call to action instead of a specific product feature allowed me to apply the same tagline across multiple adverts. Transitioning to large, high-quality lifestyle images also allowed for an efficient turnaround of multiple iterations of the same advert template promoting different products.

Placeholders for B2B client logos and contact information enabled customers to easily spot relevant information to get in touch with the audiovisual integrator.

DISTRIBUTION

Adverts were distributed across EMEA, NA and APAC geographies. They were exported for print using InDesign using a customised 240% SWOP profile for maximum compatability across a broad range of print publications.

TRADESHOW STAND DESIGN

PROJECT SCOPE

Creating visually distinctive themes allowed Future Automation's US and EMEA show stands to maintain a consistent brand presence before, during and after each tradeshow.

I worked as a part of the wider marketing team who delivered the floor plan and 3D models of each stand. My role was to design and source the large-format graphics that would be affixed to the 'walls' of the stand. 

For smaller national trade shows I create a variety of marketing collateral, ranging from touchscreen presentations using Adobe xD/Muse to simpler roller banners. These are formatted in a way that makes them applicable to multiple events and simple to transport. 

CREATIVE STRATEGY

Carrying the showstand themes across emails, social media banners and icons allowed visitors to easily identify our showstand at a distance and our social media channels.

I ensured that the unique selling points of the business I identified establishing brand guidelines were placed front and centre across the stand graphics i designed. This ensured visitors would understand our products, services, and values without requiring additional explanation from a team member.

DISTRIBUTION

Cedia and ISE tradeshows took place in EMEA and US regions. Smaller scale tradeshows are held across the UK. Social media artwork was distributed across Instagram, Facebook, Twitter and Linkedin. Emailers were created using Mailchimp and Photoshop. Show recap videos were released to youtube and shared across other platforms and on the Future Automation website. 

DIGITAL ADVERTISING & SOCIAL MEDIA

HARRIET KELSALL GOOGLE ADS

In addition to creating quarterly print campaigns, I was tasked with generating separate versions of each artwork for use in Google online advertising. This allowed Harriet Kelsall to attract a digital audience for the first time to attract younger audiences to the redesigned website.

The simplified digital adverts retained the visual themes of their printed counterparts. Maintaining a consistent visual theme was important to increase visual retention for potential customers who would be more likely to view the advert across multiple platforms. Careful planning and coordination were required to synchronise the campaigns so they could maintain their effectiveness for the three years that they were distributed.

IZABELLE HAMMON SOCIAL POSTS

Bringing visual flair to Izabelle Hammon's social media platforms gave me the opportunity to create work for an entirely different audience from those in my previous roles.

I utilised After Effects to create more eye-catching posts that would drive more engagement with the Izabelle Hammon audience.

Delivering consistent high-quality content enabled the social media coordinator to execute their strategy and grow their already established audience.

FUTURE AUTOMATION 0-10K

Building on my experience as a social media coordinator at Harriet Kelsall and producing quality content for the Izabelle Hammon team allowed me to tailor my approach to Future Automation's social strategy.

In reviewing the existing strategy, I identified that architects and designers were present on Instagram - a platform that Future Automation did not own an account.

I, therefore, championed the creation of high-quality images and videos that would appeal to these audiences on Instagram. Creating a consistent schedule using Later.com allowed me to steadily grow the Future Automation Instagram account from 0 - 10k followers. 

To grow the accounts naturally, I created a B2B media pack that I regularly updated with images and videos I shot so the network could share the latest Future Automation content with their audiences. This 'earned' strategy allowed the channel to rapidly expand and attract new clients.

CAMPAIGN DESIGN

Creating engaging pitch presentations has enabled directors and line managers to secure over £2 million of contracts with Fortune 100 businesses and celebrity clientele.

Many pitch presentations are protected by NDA's, but I have shared what I can.

In addition to pitch presentations, I was tasked at Future Automation to generate a series of webinars that any member of the sales team could deliver on a rolling basis to educate the B2B network about Future Automation's products and services.

The series of five webinars showcased a wide variety of installation scenarios to educate interior designers and architects about smart home mechanisms. Educating the audience allowed the B2B network to confidently pitch products and concepts to clients without the need for direct intervention from Future Automation.

DISTRIBUTION

Pitch presentations are designed to be both printed and digitally distributed. Inverted variants were created for more 'printer-friendly' options of darker pitch documents. 

While many pitch documents are created in InDesign, these are also often translated into Powerpoint to allow for the incorporation of video content and additional text animations.

The webinar series was built in Powerpoint with additional work in Photoshop required to deliver the required level of imagery throughout.

RESULTS

My pitch documents have contributed towards over £2 million of residential and commercial contracts with Fortune 100 and Celebrity clientele.

The webinar series has helped educate the Future Automation B2B network and has contributed to an increase in the variety of products that dealers purchase through Future Automation instead of competitors.

HARRIET KELSALL BESPOKE JEWELLERY ADVERTISING & MARKETING MATERIALS

PROJECT SCOPE

Quarterly adverts were formatted for local and national publications to generate awareness of the brand and drive new traffic to the website.

These were also adapted for Google Ads with custom links so the Directors could monitor the success of the campaigns using Google Analytics.

I also created a video campaign that was run on buses in Cambridgeshire to generate awareness of the brand among visitors and residents

CREATIVE STRATEGY

I was tasked with capturing the brief set by the company directors. Each advert had to incorporate a clear inspiration, bespoke design and showcase the ring.

Photographing and retouching the ring for each advert required expert knowledge of lighting, focus stacking, and compositing to deliver print-quality images that would stand alongside other luxury brands.

The campaigns were reviewed on a yearly basis to maintain their effectiveness and to ensure the jewellery would attract the intended target audience.

DISTRIBUTION

The print adverts were distributed locally across luxury magazines to attract the intended 34-year-old target audience. Aligning Harriet Kelsall's customers with the national average engagement age greatly increased the likelihood of an engagment ring commissions.

A side effect of the awareness campaign we had not intended was a royal commission that cemented the business as a trusted luxury brand. (See the Queen's commission on my Photography page)

IZABELLE HAMMON QUARTERLY COLLECTION CAMPAIGNS

CAMPAIGN DESIGN

This photography-themed AW17 collection required eye-catching campaign imagery to promote the upcoming release. I created posters that were shipped alongside the collection when it was released so B2B clients could actively promote the new colourways as soon as they arrived.
