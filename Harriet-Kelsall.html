Creative Direction:

THE BRIEF

Our team worked closely with an external agency that delivered a new logo and slogan for the brand.

I was tasked with developing these initial concepts into fully-fledged campaigns, a new website design and retail signage that reflected the individual nature of the brand and its customers.

Strategy Analysis:

DATA CAPTURE

To ensure our marketing efforts were targeting the relevant audience, I designed and deployed 'data capture' cards into each retail store to ensure that clients that were visiting the store reflected the average UK marriage age (34). 

Analysis of these cards showed that the target age was being achieved shortly after the rebranding initiative. This analysis helped verify the effectiveness of the signage and campaigns I created.

Design Strategy:

SIGNAGE AND 
MARKETING MATERIALS

The materials I produced showcased provenance through infographics that traced the homes of precious gemstones. I also created Diamond Charts to help non-experienced clients understand what to look for when selecting their engagement ring. These materials all aimed to instill confidence in inexperienced clients to demystify this traditionally elusive topic.

I created modern event flyers that I placed in coffee shops in the local area to generate brand awareness because of the Hertfordshires' remote location.

The retail signage I created utilised gold foiling that would glint in the sun to capture the attention of passing potential clients on the Cambridgeshire high street.

Retail tags included a 'customer' side highlighting the story, inspiration and designer of the jewellery. The rear 'showroom staff' side of each tag features more technical information to assist retail teams when speaking to customers.

Website Redesign:

A VISUAL OVERHAUL

The website relied heavily on high-quality imagery, so I created thousands of unique lifestyle photographs that encapsulated the three cornerstones of the business; bespoke design, craftsmanship and quality.

The external web development agency created 'blocks' in a variety of sizes and provided our team with access to the Umbraco content management system.

I generated and populated pages with these blocks to create the initial web pages which were then reviewed by the directors prior to the websites' launch.

Campaign Strategy:

EYE-CATCHING ADVERTISING

Quarterly adverts were formatted for local and national publications to generate awareness of the brand and drive new traffic to the website.

These were also adapted for Google Ads with custom links so the Directors could monitor the success of the campaigns using Google Analytics.

I also created a video campaign that was run on buses in Cambridgeshire to generate awareness of the brand among visitors and residents.
