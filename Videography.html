Since 2014 I've been trusted by brands to tell their individual stories through intricately crafted images and videos that best showcase their projects, products and services. Whether it's photographing a commission that was presented to the Queen, or capturing images of a world-renowned football stadium, I have the versatility and post-production workflow to deliver consistent high quality content for discerning clients.

HARRIET KELSALL BESPOKE JEWELLERY - DIGITAL TRAILER

PROJECT SCOPE

I married stock footage with my own to create an emotional story that would inspire potential clients who visited the hkjewellery website for the first time. The brief was to keep the project shorter than 30 seconds while also communicating the key performance indicators (KPIs) of the business.

SOFTWARE

After Effects (Editing), Pexels (Stock Footage)

DISTRIBUTION

Harriet Kelsall Bespoke Jewellery home page

FUTURE AUTOMATION - ABOUT US

PROJECT SCOPE

Explaining Future Automation's product offering is a daunting prospect for audiovisual integrators because of the variety of categories that are available. This project was showcased on the Future Automation 'About Us' web page and I promoted the video across social media and in a monthly newsletter.

This video also featured on displays at tradeshows to quickly showcase the company to potential clients.

SOFTWARE

After Effects (Editing, Kinetic Typography, Logo Animation), Photoshop (Image Editing)

DISTRIBUTION

Website 'About Us' page, 1- minute version for Instagram, Twitter and Linkedin, Direct download via B2B Media Pack, 4K version for tradeshow stand displays.

FUTURE AUTOMATION - MORE THAN METAL

PROJECT SCOPE

Future Automation wanted to communicate that its products are entirely manufactured in-house for existing and potential clients. 

I chose to base the video around the QA (Motorised articulating display mount) because it was an award-winning design and was the first product that Future Automation made entirely in-house in 2008.

I coordinated and filmed the multi-stage manufacturing process efficiently thanks to creating storyboards prior to filming. This gave me a shot list and schedule that I could follow to ensure the project arrived on schedule.

SOFTWARE

After Effects (Editing)

DISTRIBUTION

Future Automation website home page, B2B media pack, pitch deck presentation, factory tour presentation
