I'm a forward-thinking designer, specialising in motion, print and digital design. I've worked in-house for companies to create effective visual communication solutions to help their brands thrive for over a decade.

From a young age I was enamoured with the idea of creating something from nothing. Putting digital pen to paper studying Illustration at Portsmouth University was a natural progression for me. There, I first learned how to react to professional client briefs and understand traditional design workflows.

Since graduating in 2013, I have had to adapt and grow to fulfill the growing list of requirements of a marketing designer. Luckily, my natural passion for technology has helped me acquire a variety of technical skills that I have found invaluable in my creative career. 

When I'm not in the office you'll usually find me with a camera in my hand, rowing half marathons, or taking on overly abitious DIY projects. In my home you'll also find a small, but meaningful collection of work from artists who've inspired me.
