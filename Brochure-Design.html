CINEAIR & CINEAIR LUXE BROCHURE

PROJECT SCOPE

The brochure was created as a sales tool for external retailers when pitching the new range of rollable outdoor cinemas.

I finalised the external design of the cabinet and generated custom textures for each variation based on material swatches that I was supplied by Matthew Hill using Blender. This involved clear and consistent communication with Future Automation's Technical Direction and Commercial Director as well as the team at Matthew Hill Bespoke.

To meet the print deadline for the upcoming tradeshow launch event the brochure had to be turned around in a strict 72-hour timeframe.

IZABELLE HAMMON PRODUCT CATALOGUE

PROJECT SCOPE

Izabelle Hammon required a physical guide which showcased every product in their catalogue. I was tasked with generating page layouts, photographing/rephotographing existing products, icon/illustrative designs, cover designs, and typographic layouts. 

This was the first time Izabelle Hammon released a full product catalogue place and was welcomed by trained nail technicians across the country who could place the brochure in their salons in front of waiting customers.

FUTURE AUTOMATION 'PITCH DECK' BROCHURE

PROJECT SCOPE

It was identified that the Future Automation B2B network were looking for a holistic brochure that gave a high-level overview of Future Automation's products and services. The physical brochure would be handed out at tradeshows, one-on-one meetings, and events to serve as a 'Pitch Deck' for the network to present to their clients.

The design style of this pitch deck was carried through a series of marketing collateral (webinars, tradeshow graphics, promotional videos, microsites) to create a memorable and cohesive brand identity.

FUTURE SOUND & VISION BRAND GUIDELINES

PROJECT SCOPE

As Future Sound & Vision acquired more businesses it required a visual rebrand to distinguish itself from its growing number of subsidiaries.

I was tasked with redesigning the logo that had remained unchanged since 1998, along with establishing brand guidelines to allow the brand to expand. 

These guidelines help Future Sound & Vision explain its corporate structure to companies that it was looking to acquire. Since the rebrand, Future Sound & Vision has acquired Matthew Hill Bespoke and has significantly without confusing existing employees and subsidiaries.

FUTURE AUTOMATION PRODUCT GUIDES & TECHNICAL BROCHURES

PROJECT SCOPE

Following the company rebrand, all marketing materials were revised to ensure they matched the new company aesthetic and values.

The technical brochures needed to be designed in a way that was compatible with Solidworks. I generated an editable template that the department could import into Solidworks to export their technical product drawings without the need to use InDesign.

Clear and consistent formatting throughout the range, and an emphasis on lifestyle photography ensured these brochures would appeal to interior designers and architects looking for inspiration for future projects.
