I was headhunted to join the newly formed marketing team at Future Sound & Vision. The aim was to make their subsidiary Future Automations' products more enticing for non-technical audiences. I've created bids for over £2 million of bespoke projects, generated a portal of resources for 5000+ B2B clients and directed photoshoots for over £350 million of real estate and commercial projects.

Creative Direction:

COMPANY REBRAND

Overseeing a full rebrand of Future Automation ensured messaging was consistent at every touchpoint.

Committing brand guidelines to paper for the first time gave the business a clear and consistent vision that aligned with the directors' goals. 

This project overhauled company values, marketing strategies and brand positioning to cement the companies' position as a market-leading smart automation manufacturer.

Logo Design and Animation:

"SWITCH ON THE TV"

The logo I created is representative of an off-axis display that mimics the movement of the products that Future Automation manufactures.

It was also a shape that scaled well for social media and can be laser cut into materials if required.

Quality, In-House Manufacturing and Bespoke Design were three unique selling points our team identified that separated Future Automation from competitors.

These became the cornerstones of the marketing strategies we deployed and feature heavily throughout internal and external communications.

Each USP was simple to convey to the B2B network and easy for non-technical audiences to understand.

Media Strategy:

PHOTO/VIDEO PRODUCTION

Our team wanted to cement the brands' reputation of quality to instill confidence in clients who were unfamiliar with the brand. 

I identified that being featured in award-winning projects would be a natural way of generating a perceived level of quality for new customers. My experience as a photographer allowed our team to offer clients free images of their completed projects. 

This ensured Future Automations' products would be prominently featured throughout industry brochures and online articles when these projects were entered for awards. It also gave Future Automation a consistent stream of new content for social media, emailers, product pages, presentations and bid documents.

This strategy helped grow the dealer network to over 5000+ global partners in 40+ countries who have collectively won over 100 industry awards.

Growth Strategy:

B2B MEDIA PACK

Surveying the network showed that clients were struggling to generate content for their social media accounts to generate new leads. They were also in need of presentations and product videos to show their clients in meetings. This was limiting their expansion, and subsequently Future Automation sales.

To solve this, I created an online Media Pack that contained a wide selection of assets for dealers to use in their marketing collateral.

Dealers now had the tools they needed to confidently pitch Future Automation products to their audience. Alongside other strategies, this approach has contributed to an increase in YoY revenue from 9.5% to 15%.

Lead Generation:

BID/TENDERS

Future Automation wanted to pursue larger-scale spoke projects, but lacked the ability to generate bid/tender documentation.

I created proposal documents that Directors could use in pitch meetings to confidently communicate complex mechanisms to architects and interior designers.

NDA's limit the information that can be shared, but these projects amount to over £2 million in sales.
