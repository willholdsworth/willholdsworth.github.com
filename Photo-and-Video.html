HARRIET KELSALL BESPOKE JEWELLERY - DIGITAL TRAILER

PROJECT SCOPE

Harriet Kelsall Bespoke Jewellery commissioned a citywide bus campaign in Cambridge to showcase the company's refreshed look following a comprehensive rebrand. It was targeted toward young professionals working in Cambridge who were likely to be watching the campaign during their commute. The video was followed by comtact and location information presented by Visit Cambridgeshire to direct potential customers to the store.

I was tasked with the creation of a silent advert that communicated the 'storyteller' aspect of the company. After storyboarding concepts, I drafted the full video sequence using both paid and free stock footage to visually communicate couples going through life experiences, concluding with an engagement and wedding ceremony.

SOFTWARE

The project was created using Adobe After Effects using a widescreen aspect ratio to match the displays in the Cambridgeshire buses.

DISTRIBUTION

Cambridgeshire buses, hkjewellery.co.uk homepage

FUTURE AUTOMATION - ABOUT US

PROJECT SCOPE

Explaining Future Automation's product offering is a daunting prospect for audiovisual integrators because of the variety of categories that are available. This project was showcased on the Future Automation 'About Us' web page and I promoted the video across social media and in a monthly newsletter.

This video also featured on displays at tradeshows to quickly showcase the company to potential clients.

SOFTWARE

After Effects (Editing, Kinetic Typography, Logo Animation), Photoshop (Image Editing)

DISTRIBUTION

Website 'About Us' page, 1- minute version for Instagram, Twitter and Linkedin, Direct download via B2B Media Pack, 4K version for tradeshow stand displays.

FUTURE AUTOMATION - MORE THAN METAL

PROJECT SCOPE

After identifying in-house manufacturing as a unique selling point of Future Automation I pitched, captured, and edited a sequence showcasing the entire production of their QA2 mechanism. It was selected for its complexity of parts and because it was the first display mount the company manufactured entirely in-house. Capturing each stage across four manufacturing facilities took careful planning to ensure all processes were included.

SOFTWARE

Sony A7r ii (Capture), After Effects (Editing)

DISTRIBUTION

Future Automation website, presentations, and social media channels.
